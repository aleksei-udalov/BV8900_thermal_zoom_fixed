Thank you for choosing the Cat® S62 Pro; with MyFLIR Pro!

  • With the ability to see what the naked eye can't, MyFLIR Pro, powered by the Lepton 3.5 thermal imaging core, provides a visual image of minute temperature differences, giving users the power to see in the dark, observe invisible heat sources, compare relative temperatures, and see through smoke.
  • Created to enhance the camera device experience, MyFLIR Pro provides a simple and intuitive user interface with a variety of improved functions that make it easy to customize, collect, modify, and share useful and compelling thermal images.
