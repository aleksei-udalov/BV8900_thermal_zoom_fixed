The region of interest (ROI) measuring feature is available the same way in both Edit and Live mode. For each ROI the max/min/average temperature in the ROI is displayed.

There are two sizes of ROI available â€“ a Full Frame ROI and a Central Square ROI. Pressing and holding a ROI brings up options to delete the ROI or to show minimum and maximum temperatures.

Tap the button in the top toolbar and do one of the following:

Spotmeter ROI

  • Tap the button to create an individual Spotmeter.
  • Drag the generated Spotmeter icon across the screen to gauge the temperature of an individual Spotmeter. You can add up to five spot meters to an image.
  • Press and hold individual Spotmeter icons for options to delete the ROI

Full Frame ROI

  • Tap the button to display the max/min/average temperature for the entire scene.
  • Press and hold anywhere on the scene to bring up options to delete the ROI or to show minimum and maximum temperatures.

Central Square ROI

  • Tap the smaller icon to display the max/min/average temperature for the center of the frame.
  • The square can be resized by dragging from any corner, but it will remain centered.
  • Press and hold the center of the frame to bring up options to delete the ROI or to show minimum and maximum temperatures.
