The Isotherm feature allows you to indicate a specific temperature range with a highlight color.

  • To enable the isotherm, tap the toggle button next to the IR Scale.
  • The temperature range will default to a range in the middle of the scene temperature range.
  • Each palette has 2 recommended colors for use by the isotherm, but other colors are available. Tap the color desired for the isotherm.
  • The temperature range can be set by tapping the min and max temperature text fields
