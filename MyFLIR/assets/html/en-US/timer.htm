Note: This section applies only to the Photo and Video modes.

You can set up MyFLIR so that it saves an image after a specified interval.

Follow this procedure:

  1. Tap the icon to display the tools and settings menu.
  2. Tap the icon.
  3. Tap again to select the desired time interval (3 or 10 seconds).
  4. Tap the or button (in Photo or Video mode, respectively) to start the timer.
