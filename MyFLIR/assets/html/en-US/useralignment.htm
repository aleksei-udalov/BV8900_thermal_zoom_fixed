If the alignment of the thermal and visible cameras changes, you can make manual adjustments.

Realignment Procedure

  1. To set the alignment, tap the icon. Then tap the icon and select the Image Realignment Utility.
  2. Place an object such as your hand in the field of view, about half a meter away.
  3. Tap the camera button to freeze the image and enable alignment controls.
  4. Drag your finger along the image to adjust the alignment up and down. Use the slider to rotate it.
  5. If the object you are using to align the images is not half a meter away, set the distance with the bottom slider.
  6. Tap SAVE to apply your changes.

Revert

  • Tap REVERT to load the factory alignment.

Cancel

  • Tap CANCEL to exit the Image Realignment Utility without saving.
