You can change a variety of settings related to MyFLIR’s behavior.

Follow this procedure:

  1. Tap the icon to display the tools and settings menu.
  2. Tap the icon to display the Settings menu.
  3. On the Settings menu, you can do the following:
    • View help files and information about MyFLIR.
    • Emissivity: To change the emissivity, tap Emissivity and select one of the following:
      • Matte (Recommended).
      • Semi-Matte.
      • Semi-Glossy.
      • Glossy (Not recommended).
    • Temperature unit: To change the temperature unit, tap °C or °F.
    • Save Location: To save the GPS location in the image, swipe the slider to On.
    • Automatic calibration: To calibrate images automatically, swipe the slider to On.

What is emissivity?

Emissivity is the ability of a material to emit infrared light. To get an accurate measurement, the emissivity needs to match the material you are viewing. For most materials, the default Matte setting is a good approximation.

Glossy materials, such as metals, can give incorrect temperature measurements.
