Teledyne FLIR is the global leader in the design, manufacture, and marketing of thermal imaging infrared cameras.

Established as FLIR Systems in 1978, FLIR became synonymous with the leading edge of infrared imaging technology bringing innovative sensing solutions into daily life through thermal imaging, visible-light imaging, video analytics, measurement and diagnostic, and advanced threat detection systems.

Acquired by Teledyne Technologies in 2021, FLIR rebranded to Teledyne FLIR, and offers a diversified portfolio serving numerous applications in government & defense, industrial, and commercial markets. Our products help first responders and military personnel protect and save lives, promote efficiency within the trades, and innovate customer-facing technologies. Teledyne FLIR strives to strengthen public safety and well-being, increase energy and time efficiency, and contribute to healthy and intelligent communities.

Teledyne FLIR operates in many locations around the globe and employs a total of over 3,000 employees. Learn more at www.teledyneflir.com.
