When you save an image, it is saved in MyFLIR’s image gallery. You can at any time open an image and add a spotmeter, change the palette, etc.

To open the last taken image, tap the image thumbnail in the bottom right corner of the screen.

Image view

In Image view, you can do the following:

  • Tap the icon to go to the Library view.
  • Swipe left/right to go to the previous/next image/video.
  • (Images only.) Swipe up/down to switch between thermal and visual images.
  • (Images only.) Tap the icon to display a view where you can add a spotmeter and change the palette.
  • (Images only.) Tap the icon to display information about the image, and optionally, the GPS location where the image was taken.
  • Tap the icon to share the image/video using, e.g., e-mail, Facebook, or Twitter.
  • Tap the icon to delete the image/video.

Library view

In Library view, you can do the following:

  • Tap the icon to go to the Image view.
  • Tap a thumbnail to display the full image/video.
  • Tap and hold a thumbnail to go to Selection mode, where you can do the following:
    • Tap the thumbnails to select/deselect images/videos.
    • Tap the icon to select all images/videos.
    • Tap the icon to share the selected images/videos using, e.g., e-mail, Facebook, or Twitter.
    • Tap the icon to delete the selected images/videos.
