For phones that support accelerometer and gyroscope, you can use the panorama functionality.

You can create a panorama image by panning the camera from left to right or from right to left.

Follow this procedure:

  1. Tap the MODE button at the bottom of the screen to display the mode selector menu.
  2. Tap the icon to go to Panorama mode.
  3. (Optional step.) Tap the icon and select a different palette.
  4. Tap the button to start the panning sequence.
  5. Hold the camera steady and pan from left to right or from right to left. The app automatically detects the direction and displays guiding graphics.

  6. Tap the button to stop the sequence. If the panorama is successfully created it will be automatically saved.
