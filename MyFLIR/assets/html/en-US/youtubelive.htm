Follow this procedure:

  1. Tap the MODE button at the bottom of the screen to display the mode selector menu.
  2. Tap the icon to go to YouTube Live mode.
  3. Log in with or select your Google account and allow MyFLIR to create live videos
  4. Point the camera toward your subject of interest.
  5. Tap the button to start the live stream, wait for the button to indicate the stream has started:
  6. Tap the button to share the live stream.
  7. Tap the button to stop the live stream.
