The region of interest (ROI) measuring feature is available the same way in both Edit and Live mode. For each ROI the max/min/average temperature in the ROI can be displayed, along with markers of their location.

There are five types of ROI available â€“ Spot Meter, Rectangle (Full Frame and Movable), Circle, and Line. Pressing and holding a ROI brings up options to delete the ROI or to show minimum and maximum temperatures.

Tap the button in the top toolbar and do one of the following:

Spotmeter

  • Tap the button to create an individual Spotmeter.
  • Drag the generated Spotmeter icon across the screen to gauge the temperature of an individual Spotmeter. You can add up to five spot meters to an image.

Full Frame

  • Tap the button to display the max/min/average temperature for the entire scene.

Rectangle

  • Tap the smaller button to display the max/min/average temperature for a rectangular area of the frame.
  • The rectangle can be resized by dragging from any corner, and moved by dragging in its center.

Circle

  • Tap the button to display the max/min/average temperature for a rectangular area of the frame.
  • The circle can be resized by dragging from the edge, and moved by dragging in its center.

Line

  • Tap the icon to display the max/min/average temperature along a line in the frame.
  • To change between vertical and horizontal, double tap the line.
  • The line can be moved by dragging.
