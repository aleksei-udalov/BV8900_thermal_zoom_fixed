The IR Scale allows you to control how palette colors are applied to temperatures in the image. The IR Scale adjusts automatically by default.

  • To display the IR Scale, tap the toggle button.
  • The temperature range can be locked/unlocked by tapping the toggle button.
  • When double-tapping the IR scale the upper/lower temperature limits are reset to default automatic values.
  • Drag the scale bar to adjust the range.
  • Tap the number box to enable direct editing of the number.
