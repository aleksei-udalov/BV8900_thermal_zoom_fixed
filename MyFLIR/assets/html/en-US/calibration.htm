By default, the image is automatically calibrated to ensure an optimized image and to improve temperature accuracy. While the calibration is in progress, the image is frozen and the button will animate to indicate progress.

You can switch between automatic and manual calibration modes. In manual mode you need to manually calibrate the image.

  • To calibrate the image manually, tap the button.
  • To switch between automatic or manual mode, tap the button. Then tap the icon and toggle the setting under Automatic calibration.
